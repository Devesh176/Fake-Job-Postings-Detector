User Manual

Everything you need to know to use JobGuard effectively.

🛡️ What is JobGuard?

JobGuard is an AI-powered tool that analyzes job postings and identifies whether they are likely legitimate or fraudulent. It uses a machine learning model trained on thousands of real and fake job advertisements.

JobGuard is designed to help job seekers and HR professionals quickly flag suspicious postings. It does not replace human judgment — always do additional verification before sharing personal information with any employer.

🔍 How to Analyze a Job Posting

1

Go to the Analyze tab

Click "Analyze" in the top navigation bar to open the job analysis form.

2

Fill in the job details

Enter the job title and description — these are required. Add as many other details as available (company profile, requirements, salary). More information = more accurate prediction.

3

Set the metadata toggles

Toggle whether the posting has a company logo and screening questions. These are strong signals — real companies usually have both.

4

Optionally set your assessment

Use the "Your Assessment" toggle to mark whether you think the posting looks legitimate or fraudulent. This is saved alongside the prediction for training data quality.

5

Click "Analyze Posting"

The result appears within seconds — verdict, fraud probability, risk level, and key warning signals detected.

📊 Understanding the Results

✓

Legitimate

The model believes this is a real job posting. A low fraud probability (under 50%) means the posting matches patterns of genuine job advertisements.

!

Fraudulent

The model has detected patterns consistent with fake job postings. Exercise extreme caution — do not share personal information, bank details, or pay any fees.

Risk Levels:

LOWFraud probability below 30% — likely safe
MEDIUM30–60% — some suspicious signals, proceed with caution
HIGH60–80% — likely fraudulent, verify carefully
CRITICALAbove 80% — very likely a scam, do not engage

⚠️ Common Warning Signs

Watch out for: unrealistic salary offers, requests for personal/banking information upfront, vague job descriptions, no company profile or logo, poor grammar, requests to pay for training or equipment, and jobs that don't require an interview.

📜 Viewing History

Click the "History" tab to see all past predictions. You can review fraud probabilities, your label vs. the model's verdict, and latency. This data is stored locally and used to retrain and improve the model.

🔒 Privacy

Job posting text you submit is stored in a secure local database. It is used only for model improvement purposes. No data is sent to external servers or cloud platforms.
